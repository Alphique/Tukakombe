1. Investment Analysis & Project Appraisal

We conduct comprehensive investment and project analysis to determine commercial viability, funding suitability, and expected investor returns. Our assessments are designed to support informed decision-making by entrepreneurs, lenders, and investors.

Core Analysis Areas

  • Project feasibility and commercial viability
  • Market and demand assumptions
  • Capital expenditure and funding requirements
  • Financing structure (equity, debt, blended finance)

Financial Metrics

  • Return on Investment (ROI) analysis
  • Net Present Value (NPV) and IRR calculations
  • Breakeven analysis and payback period
  • Cash flow sustainability assessment

Risk Assessment

  • Comprehensive risk identification
  • Sensitivity and downside risk analysis
  • Scenario planning and stress testing
  • Risk mitigation strategies

2. Financial Analysis & Business Performance Review

We analyze the financial performance and position of businesses to identify strengths, weaknesses, and areas requiring improvement. This service supports strategic planning, turnaround efforts, and financing applications.

Performance Analysis

  • Historical financial performance analysis
  • Profitability and margin analysis
  • Actuals vs projections analysis
  • Operational efficiency assessment

Liquidity & Solvency

  • Liquidity assessment (working capital, cash cycles)
  • Solvency and long-term sustainability analysis
  • Cash flow performance and stress points
  • Working capital management

Structural Analysis

  • Gearing and capital structure analysis
  • Cost structures evaluation
  • Financial risk identification
  • Capital adequacy assessment

3. Financial Modelling & Projections

We develop detailed, decision-ready financial models that translate business strategies and projects into measurable financial outcomes. Our models are structured to meet the requirements of banks, investors, and development finance institutions.

Model Components

  • Integrated financial models (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
  • Multi-year financial projections
  • Project-based financial models
  • Funding and repayment modelling

Analysis Features

  • Scenario and sensitivity analysis
  • Debt service coverage and affordability analysis
  • Break-even and cash flow timing analysis
  • Investor and lender return modelling

Application Focus

  • Bank financing applications
  • Investor presentations
  • Strategic planning support
  • Performance monitoring frameworks

4. Financial Structuring & Business Formalization

We help businesses transition from informal or partially recorded operations into structured, finance-ready entities. This includes reconstructing unrecorded financial activity into credible financial statements suitable for financing and investment purposes.

Historical Reconstruction

  • Reconstruction of historical financial records
  • Conversion of unrecorded finances into formal statements
  • Normalization of revenues and expenses
  • Cash flow reconstruction and validation

Documentation

  • Preparation of management accounts
  • Formal financial statement compilation
  • Supporting schedules and documentation
  • Historical trend analysis

Financing Readiness

  • Structuring financial information for financing applications
  • Improving financial transparency and credibility
  • Compliance with reporting standards
  • Investor communication materials
