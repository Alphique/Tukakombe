Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for a Tuka Finance loan, your business should meet the following requirements:

  • Registered business operating for at least 6 months
  • Valid business registration documents
  • Bank account in business name
  • Minimum revenue threshold depending on loan type
  • Good credit history

Additional Notes

Our team reviews applications carefully and may request supplementary documents. Contact us if you are unsure about eligibility.
